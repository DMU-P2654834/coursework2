The brass section consists of instruments which are made of brass. These instruments can play much louder than other orchestral sections and have distinctive sounds. They are played by blowing into a metal mouthpiece, with the sound being created from the vibration of the player's lips. Brass instruments have valves which are pressed to change the notes being played. The instruments in this section are the trumpet (left picture), french horn, trombone (middle picture), and tuba (right picture).
