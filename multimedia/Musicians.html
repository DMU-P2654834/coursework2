Evelyn Glennie

Dame Evelyn Glennie, born 19th July 1965, is a Scottish percussionist. She has been deaf since she was 12 years old. However, this didn't stop her from becoming a world renound musician. She performs barefoot so that she can feel the music and vibrations through her feet.









James Galway

Sir James Galway, born 8th December 1939, is an Irish virtuoso flute player. He has been nicknamed "The Man With the Golden Flute". He has played in many orchestras, including the Covent Garden and the London Symphony orchestras, Royal Philharmonic and BBC orchestra.
