The London Symphony Orchestra

The video above shows the incredible London Symphony Orchestra in action. Here are some facts about this orchestra.

  • The LSO was founded in 1904

  • Its current music director is Sir Simon Rattle

  • It has 106 players across all sections

  • It was one of the first orchestras to ever make its own records and film scores.


The Vienna Philharmonic Orchestra

The video above shows the incredible Vienna Philharmonic Orchestra, which is considered to be one of the finest in the world. Here are some facts about this orchestra.

  • The Vienna Philharmonic Orchestra was founded in 1842

  • It has 145 permanent players

  • Its native name is 'Wiener Philharmoniker'

  • A permanent principle conductor has never been appointed, meaning the players themselves have more control
